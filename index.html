Projects

V2C Based ADAS

Experience the future of automotive innovation with our project, V2C (Vehicle-to-Cloud) Based ADAS Bridging Safety and Connectivity. By seamlessly integrating Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) with Raspberry Pi technology, we've created a dynamic synergy of safety and connectivity. This groundbreaking solution not only enhances real-time threat mitigation but also empowers drivers with unprecedented connectivity to AWS cloud services. The mQTT communication protocol ensures seamless data exchange between onboard systems and the cloud, optimizing communication and enabling timely updates. V2C redefines the driving experience, offering a harmonious blend of advanced safety features and unparalleled connectivity, setting the stage for a transformative journey on the road ahead.

Stop Watch

Created a Stopwatch application utilizing two 7-segment displays and two buttons for precise timekeeping, with one button to stop and another to continue timing.

Door Locker Security System

The project involves a master and slave microcontroller with SPI as communication protocol between two microcontrollers . The project aims to control smart systems to unlock doors using passwords with security aspects.

Smart Home System

Embark on a journey into the realm of smart living with our project—a Master-Slave Microcontroller System that seamlessly blends convenience and control. The Master microcontroller serves as the central hub, offering intuitive modes for opening the smart system, changing passwords, and effortlessly adding users. Complementing this, the Slave microcontroller takes charge of a myriad of functionalities, from temperature-triggered fan activation to ambient light-driven home light control. Harness the power of Bluetooth commands to effortlessly manage doors and garage access, while voice commands empower you to control the ambiance with unparalleled ease.

Distance Measuring System
